Abscissa X (Date) - a relative time of the recent update of a Web Site / a relative time of the recent update of a group of Web Sites.
Ordinate Y (Size) - may be a quantity of letters, pages, outside links, pictures, binaries, error links, or given keywords in the HTML pages of a Web Site / a group of Web Sites.
Applicate Z - an item of a list.
Brightness (in one-color worlds) - a quantity of links to the outside in the HTML pages of the Web Site.
